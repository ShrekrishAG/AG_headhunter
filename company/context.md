Accord Group creates **remarkable experiences** by rebuilding homes and building lives. We guide homeowners through insurance restoration after hailstorms, severe winds, and other natural disasters.

**Tagline:** *We Create Remarkable Experiences By Rebuilding Homes & Building Lives.*

**For candidates:** This is a high-performance sales environment in a recession-proof industry with strong earning potential and team culture.
