# Scorecard: Project Sales Representative (Resume Rubric)

**Total score: 100 points** · Score each category based **only on evidence visible in the resume**.

Reference: `Project Sales Rep Resume Scoring Rubric.pdf` · Sample: Jordan Martinez scored **77** → **Good candidate**

---

## 1. Sales Experience — 25 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| 3+ years of sales experience (B2B, home services, construction, roofing, solar, remodeling, field sales) | 10 |
| Proven ability to prospect, follow up, and close deals | 6 |
| Experience managing a sales pipeline or CRM | 4 |
| Evidence of meeting or exceeding sales goals | 5 |

**Strong indicators:** "Exceeded quota," "closed $X in revenue," "managed X leads per month," "top performer," "pipeline management."

---

## 2. Project / Field Experience — 20 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Experience selling or coordinating projects, installations, repairs, or services | 8 |
| Ability to understand customer needs and translate them into project scope | 5 |
| Experience working with operations, installers, technicians, or project managers | 4 |
| Familiarity with estimates, proposals, inspections, or site visits | 3 |

**Strong indicators:** "Managed customer projects from estimate to completion," "conducted site visits," "coordinated with crews," "prepared proposals."

---

## 3. Customer Relationship Skills — 15 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Strong customer-facing experience | 5 |
| Clear evidence of consultative selling | 4 |
| Experience handling objections, complaints, or follow-up communication | 3 |
| Positive indicators of trust-building and professionalism | 3 |

---

## 4. Communication & Professionalism — 15 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Resume is clear, organized, and professional | 4 |
| Uses measurable accomplishments instead of vague duties | 4 |
| Shows strong written communication | 3 |
| Demonstrates reliability, ownership, and follow-through | 4 |

---

## 5. Technical / Tool Fit — 10 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| CRM experience (Salesforce, HubSpot, JobNimbus, ServiceTitan, AccuLynx, etc.) | 4 |
| Comfortable with mobile apps, tablets, estimates, or digital sales tools | 3 |
| Experience with reporting, lead tracking, or pipeline updates | 3 |

---

## 6. Work Ethic & Culture Fit Signals — 10 points

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Shows persistence, self-motivation, and initiative | 4 |
| Experience in commission-based, performance-driven, or field-based roles | 3 |
| Demonstrates teamwork and coordination with internal teams | 3 |

---

## 7. Red Flags / Risk Factors — subtract up to 15 points

| Red flag | Deduction |
|----------|-----------|
| Frequent short job tenure without explanation | −3 to −6 |
| No sales experience at all | −5 to −10 |
| Resume is vague with no measurable results | −3 to −5 |
| Poor grammar, formatting, or lack of professionalism | −2 to −5 |
| Experience unrelated to customer-facing sales or project work | −3 to −8 |

---

## Final score interpretation

| Score | Recommendation |
|-------|----------------|
| **85–100** | **Strong interview** — prioritize for interview |
| **70–84** | **Good candidate** — interview if resume aligns with team needs |
| **60–69** | **Phone screen** — screen by phone first |
| **Below 60** | **Likely not a fit** — unless strong referral or unique background |
