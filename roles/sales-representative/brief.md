# Role brief: Project Sales Representative

**Job posting:** [IdealTraits — Accord Group Inc.](https://app.idealtraits.com/career/Accord-Group-Inc./350124CPGG12)

## Overview

Project Sales Representative at **The Accord Group** — uncapped commission field sales in storm restoration (roofing, siding, gutters). Help homeowners navigate insurance claims after hail and wind storms. This is **not** cold-call telemarketing — it's field sales with real opportunity and real need.

**Salary range:** $60,000 – $175,000 per year (commission-driven)  
**Location:** Market-specific (e.g. North Kansas City, MO and other TGC markets)  
**Type:** Full-time · On-site / field · Not remote

## What you'll do

- Meet homeowners after hail and wind storms
- Help them navigate the insurance claims process
- Facilitate restoration projects that bring their homes back to life
- Control your pipeline, your schedule, and your income

## Who this is for

- Former athletes
- Commission-driven sales reps
- Entrepreneurs stuck in a paycheck job
- Anyone disciplined, driven, and not afraid to knock doors and create opportunity

If you want "safe" and "steady," this isn't it. If you want upside — apply.

## What we provide

- Hands-on training
- Proven sales systems
- Best-in-class tech support
- Leadership that actually answers their phone
- A team culture built around winning

**Benefits:** Health, dental, vision insurance · Hands-on training · Career growth

## Must-have requirements (IdealTraits screening)

- Authorized to work in the United States
- Able to travel if the job requires
- Valid driver's license and full-time access to a vehicle

## Interview process

1. Apply via ZipRecruiter / IdealTraits
2. **5–10 minute phone call** — learn about you, brief role explanation
3. **In-person interview** at a local Accord office
4. Offer + onboarding
