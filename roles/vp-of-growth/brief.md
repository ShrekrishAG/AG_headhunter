# Role brief: VP of Growth & Revenue Systems

**Employer:** Doll Family Office / Accord Group  
**Indeed:** [VP of Growth & Revenue Systems — Kansas City](https://www.indeed.com/viewjob?jk=9f0097452ba376e0)  
**LinkedIn:** [VP of Growth and Revenue Systems](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/4430025098/)

## Overview

Executive operator to **design and lead the full revenue engine** — not inherit one. Build a world-class operating system that turns attention into revenue through disciplined marketing, sales, systems, coaching, and execution. Start inside Accord Group; eventually create repeatable frameworks for consulting, software, and future ventures.

**Location:** Kansas City Metro (**in office**)  
**Reports to:** CEO / Visionary  
**Compensation:** $120,000–$150,000 base + performance upside + long-term opportunity

## Mission

Own the complete enterprise engine:

**Demand Generation → Lead Management → Sales Process → Conversion → Handoff → Continuous Improvement**

Systems must produce outcomes repeatedly — not depend on heroic effort.

## What you will own

- Scalable lead generation and customer acquisition systems
- Offers, messaging, landing pages, and conversion optimization
- CRM, dashboards, scorecards, and forecasting
- Standardized presentation, financing, and close process
- Training, coaching, and accountability systems
- Driving initiatives to completion and documenting what works

## Success at 12 months

- Lead flow increases
- Conversion improves
- Rep productivity improves
- Sales management becomes measurable
- Training becomes repeatable
- Customer acquisition becomes predictable

## Ideal background

- Built a growth engine before (not just managed one)
- Managed **marketing and sales together**
- Consumer, retail, home services, franchise, or similar
- **Builder > manager · Operator > strategist · Teacher > hero · Executor > visionary**

## Interview probes

- Show a funnel you built
- Show a dashboard you used
- Show a process you implemented
- Show conversion improvements you drove
- Show what you **personally created** (not inherited)

## Must-have gates

- Authorized to work in the United States
- Willing to work **in office** in Kansas City Metro
- Demonstrated executive or director-level ownership of revenue systems
