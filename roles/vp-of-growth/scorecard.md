# VP of Growth & Revenue Systems — Resume Scoring Rubric (100 points)

Score from **visible ZipRecruiter profile text** (headline + experience). Contact may be hidden — infer only from career evidence.

## Categories

| Category | Max | What to look for |
|----------|-----|------------------|
| **Revenue / Growth Leadership** | 25 | Owned revenue outcomes; built or rebuilt a growth engine; P&L or quota accountability at director+ level |
| **Full-Funnel Systems** | 20 | End-to-end engine: demand gen → lead routing → sales process → conversion → handoff; documented playbooks |
| **Marketing + Sales Integration** | 15 | Managed or unified both functions; messaging, offers, landing pages tied to sales motion |
| **Data, Dashboards & Forecasting** | 15 | CRM ownership, scorecards, forecasting, conversion metrics, operating rhythm |
| **Training, Coaching & Execution** | 15 | Installed training, coaching, accountability; improved rep productivity; execution track record |
| **Industry & Builder Fit** | 10 | Home services, franchise, retail, consumer; operator who builds systems — not only inherited them |

**Red flags / deductions:** up to **−15** (see below)

---

## Red flags (deduct up to −15 total)

| Signal | Deduction |
|--------|-----------|
| Pure brand/marketing only — no revenue or sales systems ownership | −5 to −10 |
| Only large-enterprise politics — no hands-on builder evidence | −3 to −8 |
| Job hopping with no measurable outcomes | −3 to −6 |
| Remote-only preference / no KC ties when stated | −3 to −5 |
| Over-index on strategy decks vs shipped systems | −3 to −6 |

## Score bands

| Score | Recommendation |
|-------|----------------|
| **85–100** | **Strong interview** — prioritize; likely built engines before |
| **70–84** | **Good candidate** — interview if systems evidence aligns |
| **60–69** | **Phone screen** — probe builder vs manager; funnel/dashboard proof |
| **Below 60** | **Likely not a fit** — unless unique operator background |

**Pipeline qualify threshold:** **70+** for unlock shortlist consideration.

## Must-have gates (pass/fail signals)

- US work authorization (infer from resume if stated)
- Willingness / fit for **in-office Kansas City Metro**
- Evidence of **building** systems (not only inheriting at scale)
